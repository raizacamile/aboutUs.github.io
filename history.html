HISTORY

In the year 1970, Don Justino Sevilla donated a school named Mangga-Cacutud National High School, which is now known as Justino Sevilla High School.

Between the years 1999 and 2000, the school's name was changed to honor its donor, so from Mangga-Cacutud National High School, it became Justino Sevilla High School, named after Don Sevilla.

Jusino Sevilla High School is a public school in Arayat. This is located in Mangga-Cacutud Arayat, Pampanga. Mangga-Cacutud is a baranggay in the municipality of Arayat, province of Pampanga.
